New Interaction

Triggers

  • Hover
  • Click

    • Rules & Feedback

  • When cursor hovers over card, the card expands to reveal the title, subtitle, and description of the image.
  • The image has a red transparent overlay.
  • Two buttons appear over red overlay.
  • The two buttons turn white and the text turns red when hovered over.
  • When "Save" button is clicked, image travels into board icon.
  • "Saved to board" fades in and out underneath board icon.

    • Loops & Modes

  • There are no loops or modes.
